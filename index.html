Services

Bow Rehair Service offers bow rehairing of violin, viola, and cello bows.

  • All rehairing services include cleaning and polishing the bow, frog, and other bow components.
  • Bow rehairing prices start at $45.
  • Only high-quality, fiddler's grade bow hairs are used.

About

The most frequent repair needed by string players is the rehairing of the bow. Bow Rehair Service has a solid foundation in bow rehairing techniques and offers high-quality work using high-quality materials. Each bow rehairing service is done as quickly as possible with the highest quality materials at an affordable price.

The proprietor of Bow Rehair Service completed his bow rehairing training at the

Violin Craftsmanship Institute of the University of New Hampshire.
